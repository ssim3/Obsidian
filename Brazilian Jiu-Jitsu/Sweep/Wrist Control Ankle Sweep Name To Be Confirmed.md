Start from sitting position, opponent standing. Grab onto one of opponents wrists.

Variation 1:
![[Pasted image 20250617020126.png]]

1. Pull down on opponents wrist
2. As opponent whips back up, swiftly put feet behind their ankles, and push their wrist backwards
3. They SHOULD FALL

Variation 2:
![[Pasted image 20250617020043.png]]

1. Variation 2 comes into play when one of opponents legs are in fighting stance (Staggered)
2. Grab one ankle whilst holding the wrist
3. Push wrist between opponents legs
4. Pull ankle
5. Opponent should fall, continue to hold onto limbs to pass guard
