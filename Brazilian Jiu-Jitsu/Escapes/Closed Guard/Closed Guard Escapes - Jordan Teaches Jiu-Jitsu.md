### Survival Tips:
- ALWAYS Hip Track. Stay squared with your opponent
- Don't put arms on chest
- Don't put arms on the mat
- Don't reach back (Risk of Triangle)
- Tip: Two arms out when defending triangle allows for a double-under pass.
- BREAK GRIPS!!! Don't let them hold your lapelle or nun
- Keep Butt Down!!! Butt comes up heels = sweep
