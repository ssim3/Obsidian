Bridge & Roll:
- Start from bottom mount
- Keep feet near to butt for bridging force
- Bridge to bring opponents posture to self
- Use forearm to control elbow, other hand over collar
- step leg over opponents leg
- Bridge over shoulder (make sure to put weight on shoulder, look over shoulder)
- Roll along with the weight shift
- Top Close guard
