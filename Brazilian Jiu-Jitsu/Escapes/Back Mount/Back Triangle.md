## Keep Opponents primary choking arm in control with opposite hand

![[Pasted image 20250617165558.png]]

- Prevents opponent from choking you
- Priority number 1, if opponent strips the grip, stop everything else and stop the choke

---

## Trap opponent legs between yours

![[Pasted image 20250617170328.png]]

- Negates the body triangle control

## Use bottom hand to grab opponents top foot

![[Pasted image 20250617170524.png]]
A

- Prevents opponent from re-locking the body triangle or going for a bottom side triangle

## Square hips to ceiling and step over bottom leg

![[Pasted image 20250617170659.png]]

- Allows you to break chest to back connection and start the escape, as the triangle is now open

## Start fighting upper body seatbelt

![[Pasted image 20250617170827.png]]

- Use arms to fight and get rid of the seatbelt grip
- Start sliding back to the mat and come up on top

## If opponent is backheeling

![[Pasted image 20250617171046.png]]

- Use free arm to assist in wedging bottom knee

![[Pasted image 20250617171148.png]]

- Turn hips to ground to create ankle exposure

![[Pasted image 20250617171303.png]]

- Scissor legs and get opponents legs between yours and start escaping
