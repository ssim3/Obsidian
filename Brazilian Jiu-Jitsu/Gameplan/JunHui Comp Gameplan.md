
## Stand up Game

- Wrestle and try to stay on top
- Pass using [[J point camping]]

## Top Game

- Pass using [[J point camping]]
- Get to side control and try to cook opponent using gable grip wedge underneath opponents back, used as a rest point for me but makes opponent miserable
- Transition to knee on belly, to mount, isolate an arm
- Transition to giftwrap and chairsit backtake 
- Transition to mounted triangle
- Transition to arm triangle
### Turtle 

- If opponent turtles, try to get to front headlock and finish with [[Anaconda]]

## Bottom Game

-  Engage using [[Reverse De La Riva (RDLR)]], [[Kiss of the Dragon]]
-  Opponent tries to smash RDLR get to [[X Guard]] and wrestle up to top
-  If manage to get to [[Single Leg X (SLX)]], Lumberjack sweep and finish with straight ankle lock
- Main guard to play is [[Z guard]] and use choi bar, triangles and arm drags as main attacks
