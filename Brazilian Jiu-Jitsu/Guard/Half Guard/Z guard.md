# Objectives from Z guard
	Wrestle up
	Submit
	Leg entanglements

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
## Wrestling up

### 1. Dogfight

![[Pasted image 20250615173822.png]]

- Allows the guard player to go from bottom to a more neutral position
- Bottom player can come up on top
- #### If opponent is pushing back into you to prevent the sweep you can do a [[Sunsetter Triangle]]

### 2. John Wayne sweep

- Allows bottom player to punish overcommitment of pressure into knee shield
- Allows bottom player to come up on top

### 3. Waiter Guard Sweep

- If having trouble with dogfight position, transition to deep half, by getting scoop grip on near leg.
- Use underhook to off balance opponent forward, then swing legs around to deep half position
- Let go of underhook hand, grab far leg, and transition into waiter guard
- Sweep with a pendulum motion forward and back
- End up in knee slice to pass

## Submissions

### 1. Triangles/Arm Drags

https://youtube.com/shorts/KeFWAxeJFps?si=2dq5gP62CW8SWqBV

- If the opponent does a arm weave pass, remove top leg and go for an arm drag
- Allows you to take your opponents back or most of the time come up on top
- If your opponent is lazy and leaves their arm hanging around in an overback grip position you can shoot a triangle by controlling your opponents other hand
- Force opponent keep overback grip with a heavy overhook, preventing opponent from pulling out of the triangle threat

### 2. Choi bar

- An armbar that you can hit from bottom half guard
- Used to punish cross faces by top player
- Normally starts with a kimura grip which transitions into a [[Choi Bar]]

### 3. Leg Entanglements

- Transition to [[Reverse De La Riva (RDLR)]] pronounced Reverse Deh Lah Hiva
- Transition to [[X Guard]] to [[K Guard]] to [[Backside 5050]] and finish with a [[Belly Down Straight Ankle Lock]] or Heel hook
- Underhook far leg and threaten Single Leg X ([[Single Leg X (SLX)]]) and finish with [[Straight Ankle Lock]]
