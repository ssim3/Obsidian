One of the most popular passes.

1. Get Opponent from seated to supine
	1. Lift their ankles
	2. Ankle + Shoulder push
	3. Shoulder push
	4. Grip finding, go to opponents back

![[Pasted image 20250620033633.png]]
2. Better to push on the shins, not the knees. Bring his heels to his butt for pressure.
![[Pasted image 20250620033805.png]]
![[Pasted image 20250620034002.png]]
3. Control the hip (alternative to step 2):
	1. Pin the hip with entire elbow, not just hand to prevent frames, push all the  way in.
	2. Try to get angle from the side. Put tension on the shin before moving
![[Pasted image 20250620034115.png]]
4. Head positioning
	1. Head on chest
	2. Keep hips angled, too high and opponent may sweep, too low and theres no pressure
![[Pasted image 20250620034329.png]]
![[Pasted image 20250620034402.png]]
5. Walking back
	1. Gets rid of the elbow knee connection
